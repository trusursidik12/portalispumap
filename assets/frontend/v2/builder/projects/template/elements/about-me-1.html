Who I am

I’m Ralph Peterson, a twenty-six-year-old product designer from Los Angeles, CA, currently co-leading a design team at Intense web design studio.

The digital world is my sandbox and I’m always hungry to create new user experiences. My long-term goal is to make technology easy to use and accessible to everyone. The motivation that drives me daily is to work with people and brands that inspire me.

I love to document my journey and share most of my design work on Dribbble and Behance, my thoughts on Twitter, daily updates on Instagram and articles on Medium. When not working, I usually play anything on PlayStation or Steam.
